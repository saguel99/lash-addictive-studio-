Meet the Artist

Tammy Jean
Warren

Owner and lead artist Tammy is a Licensed Eyelash Technician in the State of Texas who started lashing at a fast-paced Los Angeles salon in 2010. In 2011, she went out on her own and opened Lash Addict's Lash Studio, LLC.

In 2014 she relocated to Anna, Texas — making Lash Addict's the first and only eyelash salon in Anna, TX. Trained and certified by industry leaders in lash, brow, permanent makeup & cosmetics, Tammy is passionate about her craft and driven by complete client satisfaction. Clients travel from DFW all the way to Oklahoma for her chair.
